A little something about me

  • I am a Software Tester in the beautiful town of Boulder, Colorado.
  • I have my own testing blog titled the Boulder Tester.
  • I’m a lifetime learner (Buccaneer Scholars unite!).
  • I run the Boulder QA Meetup.
  • I have a personal goal to learn how to code this year. I’m on my way by taking classes with GDIBoulder, learning by creating this site with Skillcrush, then learning Javascript and Python. Wish me luck!
  • One of my biggest passions is helping people do what it is they want to do whatever that is.
  • I love to teach and mentor Software Testers.
  • I believe that the best leaders are compassionate and give away what they know.
  • I believe in the art of software testing and as an ‘artist’, I constantly study my craft by reading testing books, participate in trainings, reading other tester’s blogs, participate in the Weekend Testing groups, writing, practice testing on my own time, and supporting other Testers.
